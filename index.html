Why work with a specialist

What does a Shopify developer actually do?

A Shopify developer designs, builds, and customises Shopify stores to meet your unique commercial requirements. This covers everything from theme customisation and bespoke Liquid development to third-party app integrations, custom storefront features, and performance optimisation.

At Amplify Studio, our work goes well beyond aesthetics. We focus on outcomes that matter commercially: conversion rates, average order value, and organic search visibility.

Why choose Shopify for your ecommerce store?

Shopify remains the world's leading ecommerce platform for growing brands. It's fast to launch, infinitely scalable, hosted and maintained by Shopify itself, and backed by a vast ecosystem of apps and integrations. Whether you're launching your first store or migrating from WooCommerce, Shopify provides a robust, future-proof foundation.

Shopify vs WooCommerce — which is right for you?

WooCommerce offers flexibility but comes with significant technical overhead — hosting, security, plugin conflicts, and maintenance all fall on you. Shopify handles infrastructure, security, and updates so you can focus on selling. For the majority of UK ecommerce brands, Shopify is the faster, more reliable, and more cost-effective long-term choice.

Our Shopify development approach

We start every project with a discovery phase — understanding your brand, target audience, and commercial goals. From there, we produce a clear written proposal covering scope, timeline, and a fixed price range. No vague estimates, no open-ended hourly billing.

All builds are coded to meet best practices for Core Web Vitals performance, WCAG 2.1 accessibility, and technical SEO. We don't use bloated page builders — every store we deliver is clean, well-structured, fast, and maintainable for years to come.

Technical SEO built into every build

We build stores with search in mind from day one. This includes clean URL architecture, proper heading hierarchy, meta tag structure, JSON-LD schema markup, image optimisation, and Core Web Vitals compliance. For deeper content SEO, our SEO Boost Package provides a full technical audit and roadmap.

UK-based. Transparent. Invested in your growth.

We're a UK-based Shopify development agency working with brands across the UK and internationally. All projects are handled directly — no outsourcing, no unsupervised junior developers. When you work with Amplify Studio, you deal directly with the expert building your store.

FAQ

Frequently asked
questions.

  • How long does a Shopify store build take?
    Timelines depend on scope. A Starter Store typically takes 1–2 weeks, a Growth Store 2–4 weeks, and a Premium Experience 4–8 weeks. We provide a clear timeline upfront in your written proposal.
  • Do I need a Shopify subscription before you start?
    No. We build on Shopify development stores throughout the project so you're not paying for Shopify while we're working. We'll prompt you to activate a subscription only when the store is ready to go live.
  • Can you work with my existing Shopify store?
    Absolutely. Many of our clients come with existing stores they want redesigned, optimised, or extended. We can work within your current theme or migrate you to a new one — whatever makes most sense for your goals and budget.
  • What happens after the store launches?
    All builds include a post-launch period for bug fixes. After that, you can move onto one of our monthly support plans for ongoing development, or work on a project-by-project basis.
  • Is SEO included in a store build?
    Technical SEO foundations are built into every store — clean URL structure, meta tag configuration, heading hierarchy, JSON-LD schema readiness, image alt text, and Core Web Vitals optimisation. For deeper content SEO strategy, our SEO Boost Package covers that comprehensively.
  • How does your pricing process work?
    We start with a free discovery call to understand your requirements. We then provide a written proposal with a fixed price range, scope of work, timeline, and payment terms. We don't do open-ended hourly billing.
  • Can you migrate my store from WooCommerce or another platform?
    Yes. We have experience migrating stores from WooCommerce, Squarespace, and custom-built platforms to Shopify — including product data, customer records, and 301 redirects to protect your existing SEO rankings.
  • Do you work with Shopify Plus?
    Yes. We have experience with Shopify Plus including checkout extensibility, advanced theme customisation, and B2B / wholesale setup.
